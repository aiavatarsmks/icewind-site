Cookie Policy

This policy explains the current use of cookies and similar technologies on the ICE WIND website.

Current position

ICE WIND does not currently use analytics, advertising, personalisation or other non-essential cookies in the website code. We do not use Google Analytics, Google Tag Manager, Meta Pixel, Hotjar or similar visitor-tracking tools.

Third-party services

We use Google Fonts to deliver website typography. We use FormSubmit only when you send a project enquiry through the form. FormSubmit is not loaded as a tracking script during normal browsing of the website.

These providers may process limited technical information necessary to deliver their services. Please read our Privacy Notice for more information about how personal data is processed.

Cookie consent

Because we have not identified non-essential cookies or similar technologies set by the website during normal browsing, we do not currently display a cookie-consent banner. If we introduce analytics, advertising, personalisation, embedded media, chat tools or other non-essential cookies, we will update this policy and request consent before they are set.

How to manage cookies

You can manage or delete cookies through your browser settings. Blocking cookies may affect how some third-party services work.
